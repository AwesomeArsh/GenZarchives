The GenZ Archives

By Arsh and Arianna


Lets be honest, this generation is very, very cringe. So why not make a whole dictionary stating all the slang we used?! (warning: very cringe)


The Slang Library
  • Savage- as in cool, classy, risky, dangerous
  • W- win
  • L- Lame/loser
  • Bozo- idiot
  • Ratio- having more likes than the comment you replied to (implies that you’re better than the other person)
  • Insta- instagram handle
  • TikTok- an app obsessed over
  • Snap- snapchat username
  • Devious Lick- where you deftly steal something
  • On god- an exclamation that something’s true
  • Bruh- a versatile reaction that you just say
  • Cringe- to be embarrassing, terrible
  • Stan- combination of stalker and fan, obsessed with something but not in a creepy way
  • Salty- jealous, bitter
  • Cap/No Cap- calling something a lie or fake
  • Simp- someone who overdoes it for their crush
  • Bet- another word for yes
  • (Spill the) Tea- (tell me the) gossip/drama
  • Periodt- adds emphasis or intensity, or exclaims that the point is not up for debate
  • Sus- suspicious, shady, weird
  • Slaps- describes how great/exceptional something is
  • G.O.A.T- stands for greatest of all time, is used to describe someone who is amazing at something
  • Fit check- when you post or send a picture of your outfit to someone, showing it off
  • Extra- taking something to the next level, sometimes in a bad way
  • Smol- something small, or adorable
  • Hits Different- when something feels better than usual
  • Pick me- someone who goes out of their way to impress others by “not being like the others”
  • Bop- another way of saying a song is good
  • Drip- a cool/sexy trend or style
  • Emo- someone who is overly emotional, or introverted, or someone characterized by hoods, long hair, dark clothes, and a poker face
  • ATP- stands for at this point
  • TBH- stands for to be honest
  • NGL- not gonna lie
  • Cancel- to stop supporting something or trying to stop it, usually by unfollowing, spreading bad things, or boycotting
  • ___ is over party- when a lot of people shut down or talk about how something is done/over
  • Basic- someone who likes mainstream things, not original
  • Low key- to an extent
  • I’m Dead- used when you find something hilarious or cringe
  • Vibe- a mood or emotional state that you associate with a person
  • Sis- short for sister, used to address close girl friends
  • Dms- short for direct messages
  • Cursed- provoking unsettling feelings, something uncanny/ugly
  • Ship- thinking two people would be cute/wanting them to get romantically together
  • Roast- make fun of someone
  • Speedrun- completing something as fast as possible
  • Slay- word of encouragement/appreciation
  • Oof- exclamation to sympathize with difficult situations
  • Flex- to show off something
  • Fire- fashionable, tasteful, great
  • Hella- slang for really
  • Hella- slang for really
  • Hype- crazy/energetic
  • Pop/Go Off- when you are doing something amazing or in an intense fight in a game and win (“person popped off”)
  • Must be nice- a passive aggressive saying of jealousy
  • Shook- being surprised or in shock from something
  • Trolling- hacking or malicious online behavior
  • Lol- laugh out loud, an exclamation that somethings funny
  • 4k- when you do something sus and you get caught in clear view
  • Bussin - when something is amazingly tasty
  • Kk- short for okay
  • Waifu - hot anime girls who you want to marry
  • Bias- favorite in a group
  • Weeb - anime addicts
  • Anti- a person opposed to something or someone
  • Hater- someone who always criticizes or insults something
  • Comeback- When an artist releases a new song/album
  • Bias-Wrecker- someone in a group who’s a second favorite, who sometimes overtakes the initial bias
  • Binge- to watch a lot of a show
  • Shade- subtle verbal contempt for someone
  • Thirsty- When someone is desperate for a girls/boys attention
  • AF- stands for ‘as f**k’
  • Bye- an exclamation of being done with something or telling someone to get out/go away
  • Crying Kicking Screaming (eye stabbing)- anger- when you throw a tantrum because of something
  • Ilysm- I love you so much
  • @- someone’s username on social media
  • Fr- for real
  • Yas (Queen)- basically saying pop off
